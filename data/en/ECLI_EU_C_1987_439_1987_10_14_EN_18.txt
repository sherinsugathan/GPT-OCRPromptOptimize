

On those grounds

COMMISSION v DENMARK



THE COURT


hereby:

(1) 	Declares that by not adopting all the laws, regulations and administrative provisions necessary to comply with Council Directive 79/831/EEC of 18 September 1979 amending for the sixth time Directive 67/548/EEC on the approximation of the laws, regulations and administrative provisions relating to the classification, packaging and labelling of dangerous substances, the Kingdom of Denmark has failed to fulfil its obligations under the Treaty;

(2) Orders the Kingdom of Denmark to hear the costs.
